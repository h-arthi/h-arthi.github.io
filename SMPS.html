OVERVIEW

The main aim of this project was to provide practical experience in the design of power electronics and control through the design, construction, and testing of a two-switch forward converter based on a given specification. This project was a collaboration between two other students and myself.

PROJECT OBJECTIVES

Based on the overiview above, the prject was divided into several objectives as follows:

  • Simulation of Open Loop Converter, Gate Driver & PWM Circuit and the Magnetic Design & PCB Design for converter
  • Gate drive and PWM circuit construction and testing, populate and test the PCB
  • Closed Loop Output Voltage Control simulation and construction
